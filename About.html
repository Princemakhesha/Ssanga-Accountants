About Us

Who We Are

SSanga Accountants is a professional accounting and compliance firm based in South Africa, dedicated to empowering businesses through financial clarity and regulatory compliance. Our team brings a wealth of experience in supporting small and medium-sized enterprises with transparent, accurate, and forward-thinking financial strategies.

Since our inception, we've helped clients across various sectors manage their accounting, taxation, payroll, and statutory requirements with confidence. We believe in building long-term relationships through trust, integrity, and consistent delivery of quality service.

What We Do

Our services range from day-to-day bookkeeping and payroll processing to comprehensive tax planning, company registrations, and statutory compliance. We use modern tools and technologies to ensure that our clients always have a clear view of their financial position.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in accounting and compliance for SMEs in Africa by offering personalized, results-driven services that empower business growth.

Our Mission

To help businesses succeed by taking the stress out of financial management, ensuring compliance, and providing expert support when it's needed most.

Meet Our CEO

Dr. Masanga Hamza (MUFI-SAAB) Owenjovu, the founder and Chief Executive Officer of SSanga Accountants, brings over 15 years of industry experience. With a deep understanding of the local regulatory environment and a passion for supporting entrepreneurship, Samuel has led the firm to become a reliable partner to clients across the country.

His leadership is defined by transparency, continuous learning, and a client-first approach. He believes that empowering businesses with the right financial tools and knowledge is key to economic growth and long-term sustainability.
