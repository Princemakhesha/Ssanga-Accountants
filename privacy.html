Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

At SSanga Accountants, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or contact us through our online forms.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you use our website:

  • Name

This information is collected through our contact form and is used solely to respond to your queries and offer our professional accounting services.

2. Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects non-personally identifiable information such as:

This data is used to improve our website experience and services. All data collected by Google Analytics is subject to Google's own privacy policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used only for:

  • Responding to your inquiries

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or with your express consent.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
